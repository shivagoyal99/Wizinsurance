My Background

I hold a Master of Engineering (MEng) and spent years as a structural engineer — designing buildings, calculating load tolerances, and making sure every structure could withstand real-world pressure. That training gave me one thing that's rare in financial services: a genuine obsession with precision.

Before moving into insurance and financial planning, I served in 3 different government positions. That experience gave me a thorough, practical understanding of how Canadian government benefit programs work — CPP, OAS, GIS, RDSP, CESG, the Canadian Dental Care Plan — and how they interact with private insurance and financial planning.

Why I Do This

I became an independent advisor because I wanted to compare the market honestly — not sell one company's products. Working with 25+ carriers means I can find you the right coverage at the right price, every time. No pressure. No commission-driven shortcuts. Just the best policy for your situation, engineered with care.

With 20+ five-star reviews and 50+ families protected across Canada, my reputation is built on trust, transparency, and genuine results. When you call me, you reach me — not a call centre.

My Unique Advantage

Most advisors know insurance products. I also bring a structural engineer's analytical precision and a thorough understanding of the Canadian benefits landscape. Together, that means I can design a plan that coordinates your private coverage with the government programs you're already entitled to — resulting in comprehensive protection without unnecessary overlap or gaps.
