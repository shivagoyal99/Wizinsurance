Universal Life Insurance

The most flexible permanent insurance product. Adjustable premiums, adjustable coverage, and a built-in investment component.

📞 Call (647) 995-5891 💬 WhatsApp 📋 Send a Message

How Universal Life Works

Universal life (UL) separates the insurance and savings components of a policy. You pay a premium that covers the cost of insurance, and any excess is deposited into a tax-sheltered investment account within the policy.

  • Flexible premiums — pay more or less within limits
  • Adjustable death benefit as needs change
  • Investment component grows tax-sheltered
  • Choose from conservative to equity-linked investments
  • Policy loans available
  • Can be structured for maximum tax efficiency

💼 Common Uses

  • Corporate-owned insurance for business owners
  • Tax-sheltered savings beyond RRSP/TFSA limits
  • Estate equalization between beneficiaries
  • Key person insurance for corporations

⚙️ Complexity Requires Expertise

Universal life is a powerful tool but requires careful design. I model different premium scenarios so you understand exactly how your policy will perform over time.
