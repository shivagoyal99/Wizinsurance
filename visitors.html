Who Needs Visitors Insurance?

Canada's healthcare system does not cover visitors, new immigrants, or people on work/study permits who haven't yet qualified for provincial health coverage. A single emergency room visit in Canada can cost $3,000–$10,000+ without insurance.

  • Tourists and family visitors to Canada
  • New immigrants waiting for provincial health coverage
  • Returning Canadians not yet covered by OHIP
  • Work permit and study permit holders
  • People between jobs or coverage gaps

📋 What's Covered?

  • Emergency hospitalization & surgery
  • Doctor visits & specialist referrals
  • Ambulance services
  • Prescription drugs (emergency)
  • Dental emergencies
  • Medical evacuation & repatriation

Frequently Asked Questions

Do visitors to Canada need health insurance?
Yes. Visitors to Canada are not covered by any provincial health plan. Without insurance, a single emergency room visit can cost $3,000–$10,000 or more out of pocket.
How much coverage do I need?
Most advisors recommend a minimum of $100,000. For longer stays or older visitors, $150,000–$300,000 gives better peace of mind and often isn't much more expensive.
Can I buy coverage after arriving in Canada?
Yes, but there's usually a waiting period of 48–72 hours before coverage begins. It's always better to buy before departure to ensure full coverage from day one.
Are pre-existing conditions covered?
Some plans cover stable pre-existing conditions. The definition of "stable" varies by insurer — I'll review your situation and find the right plan that covers your specific health history.
What does Visitors to Canada Insurance cover?
It typically covers emergency hospitalization, physician visits, ambulance, prescription drugs during emergencies, dental emergencies, and repatriation. Coverage limits and inclusions vary by plan.
Can I extend my visitors insurance policy?
Yes, most policies can be extended before they expire, as long as no claims have been made and the visitor hasn't left Canada. It's important to extend before the policy ends — not after.
Is visitors insurance refundable if they leave early?
Most policies offer a pro-rated refund for unused days if the visitor returns home early and no claims have been made. Terms vary by insurer.
How quickly can a policy be issued?
In most cases, same-day. I can get a policy issued and emailed to you within hours of your request.
